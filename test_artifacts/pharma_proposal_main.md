# Commercial Proposal: Global Clinical Analytics & AI Factory Platform

## 1. Project Background
The objective is to assemble an enterprise-wide cloud data lake to ingest, clean, and process historical and active clinical trial results under strict GxP (Good Clinical Practice) data integrity guidelines.

## 2. Global Client Sharding & Strategy Tags
* **Client Sector:** Healthcare & Pharmaceuticals
* **Operational Tags:** `#GxP-Compliance`, `#Multi-Region-PII`, `#Fixed-Price-Delivery`

## 3. Commercial Framework & Cost Structure
This engagement is structured strictly as a Fixed-Price delivery model capped at £2,400,000.
* **Infrastructure Budget:** The fixed budget allocates a maximum of £15,000 per month for cloud hosting infrastructure, including computing, storage, network ingress/egress, and advanced firewall tools. Any operational overages will be absorbed by the delivery consulting team during the implementation run.

## 4. Delivery Resource Allocations
A lean squad consisting of 1 Lead Data Engineer, 1 Cloud Architect, and 2 Junior Developers will execute the ingestion pipelines over a 6-month lifecycle.
